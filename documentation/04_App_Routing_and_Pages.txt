APP ROUTING & PAGE BREAKDOWN
============================

1. MAIN NAVIGATION ROUTES
-------------------------
- / (HOMEPAGE): The landing page featuring a cinematic hero section, featured artists, and an introduction to the museum's mission.
- /GALLERY: A grid-based view of available artworks with advanced filtering options (category, technique, availability).
- /ARTIST: Detailed portfolios of showcased artists, including biographies and gallery highlights.
- /EXHIBITIONS: Information on current, upcoming, and past events held at the museum.
- /COLLECTION: Curated sets of artworks grouped by theme or historical significance.

2. USER UTILITY ROUTES
----------------------
- /LOGIN & /SIGNUP: Authentication entry points for visitors and artists.
- /ABOUT: High-level overview of the library, the team, and the mission.
- /CONTACT: Support channels and inquiry forms.
- /VISIT: Practical information for physical museum visits (maps, hours, ticketing).
- /HELP: Documentation and FAQ for users of the platform.

3. TRANSACTIONAL & LEGAL ROUTES
-------------------------------
- /CHECKOUT: Secure multi-step process for purchasing tickets or acquiring art.
- /PRIVACY & /TERMS: Mandatory legal documentation and compliance notices.
- /ACCOUNT-SUSPENDED: State-specific landing page for restricted accounts.

4. ADMINISTRATIVE & TECHNICAL ROUTES
------------------------------------
- /DASHBOARD: The root of the management portal (requires admin/artist privileges).
    - /dashboard/admin/users: Management of registered members.
    - /dashboard/analytics: Real-time visualization of site traffic and sales.
- /SEND-NOTIFICATION: Admin tool for triggering push notifications to the user base.
- /TEST-NOTIFICATIONS: Sandbox environment for verifying push notification delivery and formatting.

5. CORE LAYOUT FILES
--------------------
- layout.tsx: Manages global state, providers (Theme, Auth), and persistent UI elements like the Navigation and Footer.
- error.tsx & global-error.tsx: Error boundary implementations for graceful failure handling.
- not-found.tsx: Custom 404 page maintaining the brand's aesthetic.
